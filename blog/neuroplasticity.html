Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

Growth mindset is the way thinking towards any challenges in life. Having a growth mindset is being inspired by others’ success and not compare and put ourself down for it. Having a growth mindset is continuing to persevere is the face of hardship and not give up when it’s too hard. This is useful in this course because there will definitely be a lot of hardships coming our way and it is important to have that growth mindset to keep going and push through the challenges to achieve success.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

It made me realise how much of a fixed mindset I had this whole time and that is why I have not found much success in life. I thought I was just a failure as a person and it is what it is but it was actually myself who has been holding me back. My mindset was that I would put in as little effort into things and if it doesn’t work out well then at least I have en excuse of my failure because I thought I would rather have that than put in effort and still fail. But having a growth mindset means that failure is okay and I will try to keep practicing and do better next time.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

I will face any upcoming challenges and remind myself that I have -yet- to learn the content. I will eventually understand it and if I don’t yet, that is ok as long as I keep trying and don’t give up.I will learn from any criticisms I get and do better through that guidance. I will continue to put in effort no matter how unmotivated I am and find a way to complete the goal. I think having a “I’ll do it later” mindset is also bad so I should face that biggest challenge in my life and do it NOW.
