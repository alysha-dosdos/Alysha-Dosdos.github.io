Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It is the ability to understand yourself and others, their feelings and their emotion. Being able to make decisions based off this knowledge about people is emotional intelligence. It is the ability to be aware of how you're feeling and how to manage those feelings so that we don't act on our emotions and stay calm in any situation. The same goes when it comes to how others are feeling and being able to help or communicate and defuse any situation is important in any social setting.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is about the knowledge of people in relationships and how humans react to things whereas IQ is about the mental capabilities to deal with complex problems about the world as a whole. IQ is mostly the knowledge within ourselves and how we solve problems logically whereas EQ is the knowledge about managing emotions and how we can understand our own and other's feelings.

Why is EQ important?

It is important to build and maintain relationships with others and yourself, to understand and communicate any difficulties. Without EQ, it will be hard to deal with complex problems with troubling emotions. When we speak out with anger, we don't end up seeing the logic or see the other side's perspective which is important when it comes to solving an issue with someone else. This is why having the ability to self regulate and also empathize with someone else can help build relationships with others which allows us to work with anyone in a team, especially in tech.
