Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It is the ability to understand yourself and others, their feelings and their emotion. Being able to make decisions based off this knowledge about people is emotional intelligence.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is about the knowledge of people in relationships and how humans react to things whereas IQ is about the mental capabilities to deal with complex problems about the world as a whole.

Why is EQ important?

It is important to build and maintain relationships with others and yourself, to understand and communicate any difficulties. Without EQ, it will be hard to deal with complex problems with troubling emotions.
