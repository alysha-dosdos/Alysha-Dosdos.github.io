Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

I will do high intensity training for at least 15 minutes everyday. I will also eat a more nutritional diet full of fruits and vegetables, vitamins, fibre and protein. I will also have a proper and consistent sleep schedule with at least 8 hours of sleep every night.

I will spend time with my parents every sunday for church and for lunch, meet with my boyfriend at least twice a week and catch up with a friend at least once a week. I will lend an ear to anyone who needs it so that they can also unload some burden in their lives and we can help each other.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

I will spend at least an hour a day away from social media and mindless distractions, this can be spent doing the yoga/meditation, other hobbies or with people. I will ask for help when I need it and talk to someone about my feelings in life. I won't bottle up my emotions anymore and communicate with everyone.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

I will do yoga/meditation after work, before I start working on the sprint. I will stop and practice appreciation and gratefulness every morning and reflect at night. I will also pray at church for my family and friends, my continued motivations in life and future success for everyone.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

I will go for a hike at least once a week and go for a walk by the ocean to appreciate nature's beauty in the mountains and the sea. I will try to appreciate the little things in nature such as the breeze, the sunlight against my skin and the clouds above. I'll try to look at some greenery where I can and maybe try to raise a plant.
